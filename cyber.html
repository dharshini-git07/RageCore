About This Game

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure RPG set in Night City, where you play as V, a cyberpunk mercenary on the hunt for a unique implant that holds the secret to immortality.

Customize your character's cyberware, skills, and playstyle, then take on jobs and gigs across a vertical, densely detailed city with multiple ways to approach nearly every encounter.

  • Deep character customization and cyberware builds
  • Branching story shaped by player choices
  • Dense, vertical open world in Night City
  • Phantom Liberty expansion adds a new district and storyline
