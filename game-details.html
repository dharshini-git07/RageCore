About This Game

Rise, Tarnished, and be guided by grace to brandish the power of the Elden Ring and become an Elden Lord in the Lands Between. Explore a seamless open world where every horizon hides a dungeon, ruin, or legacy castle worth investigating, and shape your build from a deep pool of weapons, spells, and armor.

Face towering bosses alone or summon allies for cooperative play, and piece together a story told through item descriptions, environmental detail, and conversations with the Lands Between's scattered inhabitants.

  • Massive open world with horseback exploration
  • Deep character building across classes, weapons, and spells
  • Challenging boss encounters, solo or in co-op
  • Optional online play including invasions and summons
