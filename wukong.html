About This Game

Black Myth: Wukong reimagines the legend of the Monkey King through breathtaking environments, punishing boss encounters, and a combat system that rewards precise timing and creative use of transformation abilities.

Traverse richly detailed levels drawn from Chinese folklore, uncovering hidden lore and secrets as you push toward some of the most memorable boss fights in recent action RPGs.

  • Deep, transformation-based combat system
  • Environments and bosses drawn from Chinese mythology
  • Richly detailed, cinematic level design
  • Challenging boss encounters with varied strategies
